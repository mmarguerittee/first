The Meaning of Peonies in Bouquets

Peonies are often seen as a symbol of romance, prosperity, and good fortune. Their lush, full blooms represent beauty, love, and honor, making them a popular choice for wedding and celebration bouquets. In many cultures, peonies also stand for new beginnings and lasting happiness. Whether given as a gift or used for decoration, peonies bring elegance and a sense of positive energy to any bouquet.
